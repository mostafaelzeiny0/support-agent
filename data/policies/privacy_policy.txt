# Privacy Policy for EasyMart

**Effective Date: [Current Date]**

## 1. Data Collection

EasyMart collects personal information necessary to provide quality service, including:
- Names and contact information (email, phone, address)
- Billing and shipping addresses
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
- Order history and preferences

We collect data only when you voluntarily provide it during account creation, checkout, or customer service interactions.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your information enables us to:
- Process and deliver orders
- Send order confirmations and updates
- Provide customer support
- Improve our services and user experience
- Send promotional communications (with your consent)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

EasyMart shares personal data only with trusted partners:
- **Fulfillment partners**: Your shipping address for delivery
- **Payment processors**: Your payment information for secure transactions

We never sell customer data to marketers or advertisers. All partners must maintain equivalent privacy standards.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:
- SSL encryption for all data transmission
- Secure payment processing compliance (PCI-DSS)
- Limited employee access to personal information
- Regular security audits and updates

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request data removal (subject to legal requirements)
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime

Submit requests via our privacy contact below.

## 6. Cookie Usage

EasyMart uses cookies to:
- Remember login information
- Track browsing preferences
- Analyze site performance
- Enhance your shopping experience

You may adjust cookie settings in your browser preferences.
