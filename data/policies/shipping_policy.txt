# EasyMart Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options

**Standard Shipping**
Delivery within 5-7 business days. Perfect for planned purchases and everyday items. Cost: $5.99 (complimentary on orders $50+)

**Expedited Shipping**
Need your items faster? Choose expedited shipping for guaranteed 2-3 business day delivery. Cost: $12.99

## Free Shipping

Orders totaling $50 or more qualify for free standard shipping. No code needed—discount applies automatically at checkout.

## Shipping Costs

- Standard Shipping: $5.99
- Expedited Shipping: $12.99
- Free Shipping: Orders $50+

*Note: Shipping costs calculated at checkout based on destination and selected method.*

## Tracking Your Order

Every shipment includes a tracking number sent via email once your order ships. Monitor your package's journey in real-time through our website or your carrier's tracking portal.

## International Shipping

EasyMart ships to 100+ countries worldwide. International delivery typically takes 10-21 business days depending on location. Customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties or import taxes.

## Shipping Details

- Orders process within 1 business day
- Weekends and holidays excluded from processing times
- Remote areas may experience extended delivery
- Delivery addresses must match billing information

## Questions?

Contact our customer service team at support@EasyMart.com or call 1-800-NOVA-MART for shipping inquiries.

*Policy effective January 2024. Subject to change without notice.*
