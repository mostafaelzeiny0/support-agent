# EasyMart Return Policy

## Overview
We want you to love every purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

## Return Window
You have **30 days from your purchase date** to initiate a return. We count from when your order arrives at your door, not when you ordered it.

## Return Conditions
To qualify for a return, items must be:
- Unused and in new condition
- In original packaging and with all tags attached
- Include any accessories or materials that came with the product
- Free from damage caused by misuse

## How to Start a Return
1. Log into your EasyMart account and select "My Orders"
2. Choose the item you'd like to return and click "Return Request"
3. Select your reason and follow the prompts
4. Print your prepaid return shipping label
5. Pack securely and drop off at any carrier location

We'll email you tracking information for your return shipment.

## What Can't Be Returned
- Personalized or custom items (engraved products, monogrammed goods)
- Digital downloads or software
- Opened beauty and personal care items
- Items purchased during final sale events
- Products damaged by customer misuse

## Refunds
Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within **5-7 business days**. Refunds go back to your original payment method. Please allow 3-5 additional business days for your bank to process the credit.

**Note:** Return shipping is free on most items. However, certain oversized items (furniture, large appliances) may require a shipping fee deducted from your refund.

## Exchanges
Prefer a different size or color? Exchanges are free! Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" during checkout, and we'll send your replacement before requesting the original item back.

## Questions?
Contact our friendly support team at support@EasyMart.com or use our live chat—we're here to help!
