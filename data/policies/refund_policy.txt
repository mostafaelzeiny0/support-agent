# EasyMart Refund Policy

## Overview
At EasyMart, we stand behind our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward refund process to ensure your peace of mind.

## Full Refunds
Items in original, good condition are eligible for full refunds. Products must be unused, unwashed, and in resalable condition with all original tags and packaging intact. To initiate a return, contact our customer service team within 30 days of purchase.

## Refund Processing Time
Once your returned item is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when your refund is approved and processed.

## Refund Method
All refunds are issued to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, or digital wallet). Please allow additional time for your financial institution to post the credit to your account.

## Refund Amount Thresholds

**Refunds up to $150:** Automatically processed without additional review.

**Refunds exceeding $150:** Require escalation to a support specialist for verification and approval. These refunds may take additional time for processing.

## Partial Refunds
Items showing signs of damage, excessive wear, or missing components are eligible for partial refunds. Our inspection team will assess the damage and determine an appropriate refund amount, typically ranging from 25-75% of the original purchase price.

## Shipping Refunds
- **Standard Shipping:** Non-refundable
- **Expedited Shipping:** Refundable if the return is initiated within 14 days of purchase

## Questions?
For refund inquiries or to initiate a return, contact EasyMart Customer Service at support@EasyMart.com or 1-800-EasyMart.
