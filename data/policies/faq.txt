# EasyMart FAQ Document

## Returns and Refunds

**Q1: What is EasyMart's return policy?**
A: Most items purchased from EasyMart can be returned within 30 days of purchase in their original condition with all packaging and accessories included. Items must be unused and in resalable condition. Some exceptions apply to clearance items, perishables, and personalized products, which are typically non-returnable.

**Q2: How do I initiate a return?**
A: Log into your EasyMart account, navigate to "My Orders," select the item you wish to return, and click "Return Item." Follow the prompts to provide a reason and print your prepaid shipping label. Once processed, your item will be returned to our warehouse.

**Q3: How long does it take to receive my refund?**
A: After we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days. The refund amount will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow an additional 3-5 business days for the credit to appear depending on your financial institution.

**Q4: What is EasyMart's policy on defective or damaged items?**
A: If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact our customer service team within 14 days with photos as evidence. We'll either send a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund. No return shipping is necessary for defective items—we'll provide a prepaid return label or authorize you to dispose of the item.

## Shipping and Delivery

**Q5: What shipping options does EasyMart offer?**
A: We offer Standard Shipping (5-7 business days), Express Shipping (2-3 business days), and Overnight Shipping (next business day). Shipping costs vary by method, order weight, and destination. Free Standard Shipping is available on orders over $50.

**Q6: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, EasyMart ships to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-21 business days depending on the destination. Customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties and import taxes. Visit our shipping calculator for specific rates and timeframes.

**Q7: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
A: If your order hasn't entered our warehouse for packing, you can change the shipping address by contacting customer service immediately. Once packing has begun, address changes cannot be made. You'll need to refuse delivery and place a new order with the correct address.

## Order Status and Tracking

**Q8: How can I track my order?**
A: After your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and carrier information. You can also log into your EasyMart account and view real-time tracking updates under "My Orders." Tracking information typically becomes available within 24 hours of shipment.

**Q9: My tracking number shows my package was delivered, but I didn't receive it. What should I do?**
A: Check with neighbors, family members, or others at your delivery location. If the package is truly missing, contact EasyMart customer service within 48 hours with your order and tracking numbers. We'll investigate with the carrier and either send a replacement or issue a refund.

**Q10: Why hasn't my order shipped yet?**
A: Most orders ship within 24-48 hours of purchase. Orders placed on weekends or holidays may take longer. High-demand periods may cause slight delays. If your order hasn't shipped within 3 business days, check your email for any holds or issues, or contact customer service for an update.

## Payment and Pricing

**Q11: What payment methods does EasyMart accept?**
A: EasyMart accepts all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and EasyMart gift cards. We also offer installment payment options through Klarna and Affirm for purchases over $50.

**Q12: Why was I charged differently than the displayed price?**
A: The price shown may not include applicable sales tax based on your shipping address. If you used a coupon or promo code, ensure it was properly applied at checkout. If you still have concerns, contact customer service with your order number and we'll review the charges.
